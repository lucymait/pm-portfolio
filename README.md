# Lucy Maitland — PM Portfolio

Product management case study built across the Udacity PM Nanodegree, completed April 2026.

---

## The Project

**LinkedIn Career Growth Platform** — an AI-powered skill gap analysis and career development tool for mid-career professionals, designed as a new feature within LinkedIn.

The platform connects LinkedIn's profile data, live job market signals, and learning content into a single unified career growth journey — helping users identify which skills matter most for their next role, close those gaps, and track readiness over time.

---

## Four Submissions, One End-to-End Case Study

| # | Submission | What it covers |
|---|-----------|----------------|
| 01 | **Design Sprint** | HMW exercises, solution sketches, 7-screen Figma prototype (V1 + V2), user research, iteration |
| 02 | **Managing Product Development** | Test plan, dogfood survey, issue prioritisation, Go/No Go meeting, engineering handoff |
| 03 | **Deliver a Product to Market** | Full PRD, pricing strategy, acquisition channels, launch plan, post-launch A/B test design |
| 04 | **Business Case & Pitch** | Market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), competitive analysis, revenue projections, 3-year roadmap |
